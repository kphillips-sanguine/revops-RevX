# Business Glossary — Sanguine Bio

## Company Terms

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| **Sanguine Bio** | Biotech company connecting patients with clinical research opportunities. Facilitates biological sample collection for pharma/biotech research. |
| **RevOps** | Revenue Operations — the team responsible for the technology stack supporting sales, marketing, and customer success. Primary platform: Salesforce. |
| **Partner Site** | External research location that works with Sanguine Bio to collect samples. Has its own staff, protocols, and patients/donors. |
| **Donor** | Patient or individual providing biological samples (blood, tissue, etc.) for research. |
| **Study** | A clinical research project or trial that requires biological samples. Managed by pharma/biotech sponsors. |
| **Sponsor** | Pharmaceutical or biotech company funding a study and requesting samples. |
| **Sample** | Biological specimen (blood, tissue, etc.) collected from a donor for research purposes. |
| **Kit** | Collection kit sent to partner sites containing supplies needed to collect and ship samples. |

## Technology Terms

| Term | Definition |
|------|-----------|
| **RevX** | AI team assistant (OpenClaw) for the RevOps engineering team. Manages projects, tracks cases, coordinates development. |
| **Code Monkey** | AI development agent that writes Salesforce code (Apex, LWC). Spawned by RevX for coding tasks. |
| **Spec Ops** | AI requirements agent that gathers and documents project specifications. |
| **n8n** | Open-source workflow automation tool used for integrations between Salesforce and external services. |
| **Experience Cloud** | Salesforce feature for building external-facing portals (partner sites, donor portals). |
| **sfdx-git-delta** | Tool that generates minimal Salesforce deployment packages by comparing git commits. |
| **Platform Event** | Salesforce real-time messaging system used for event-driven architecture. |
