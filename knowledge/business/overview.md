# Sanguine Bio — Company Overview

## What We Do

Sanguine Bio is a biotech company that connects patients with clinical research opportunities. We facilitate biological sample collection (blood, tissue, etc.) for pharmaceutical and biotech research partners.

## Key Business Processes

### Sample Intake
The process of receiving and processing biological samples from donors/patients. Tracked in Salesforce through Cases and custom objects.

### Partner Site Portal
External-facing portal for research partner sites to manage their interactions with Sanguine Bio. Built on Salesforce Experience Cloud.

### Revenue Operations (RevOps)
The team responsible for the technology stack that supports sales, marketing, and customer success operations. Primary platform: Salesforce.

## Technology Stack

- **CRM / Platform:** Salesforce (Sales Cloud, Service Cloud, Experience Cloud)
- **Automation:** Salesforce Flows, Apex triggers, n8n workflows
- **CI/CD:** GitHub Actions, SF CLI, sfdx-git-delta
- **AI Agents:** OpenClaw (RevX), AgentForge (internal platform)
- **Communication:** Slack
