Seismic Learning Feedback
Got feedback?
Should you have any immediate questions or see something confusing or out of date as
you go through a lesson, Seismic gives you two ways to be heard!
Option 1: Highlight and Send with Micro-feedback
Micro-feedback allows you to highlight text within the lesson and send specific feedback to
an Expert related to that text.
How to use: When you highlight text within a Lesson, a pop up tooltip will appear. After
clicking the 'Send Feedback' button on the pop up tooltip, Seismic Learning will auto-
populate the text you highlight and allow you to enter custom feedback. Once you click
Send, your question and the text related to it, will be sent to that lesson's expert to
respond.
Pro Tip: Micro-feedback allows you not only to get specific answers to your
questions, but to help keep our Seismic Learning content helpful, clear, and up-to-
date!
Option 2: Send general questions to Ask the Expert
If you have a general question on a section within a lesson, you can simply click on the
floating 'Ask the Expert' bubble to email your comment to an Expert, who will respond
shortly.
