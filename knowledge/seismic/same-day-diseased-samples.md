Overview
Same-Day Delivery for Diseased Samples
Sanguine has the ability to deliver patient samples the same-day for select diseases and
locations. Feasibility will assess each opportunity but the main components are the
following:
• Disease Type (how prevelant)
◦We will never do same-day for rare diseases
• How many patients we have for that disease in the area
• Do we have good phlebotomy coverage?
• Is there any I/E that will hinder the ability to find these patients?
We identify donors within 4 hours of the client's laboratory.

Personas
Personas
Many Scientists/Researchers who need same-day delivery are looking at certain elements
of the blood that degrade quickly with time (i.e. Proteins)
Fresh whole blood gives researchers the ability to generate high-quality, reproducible
results. Especially for accurately analyzing those targets of interest that may be:
• low at a baseline level
• transiently expressed
• have a short half-life in circulation

Value
Value
Same-day biospecimen collections by professional phlebotomists provide the following
value:
• Fresh, Whole Blood delivered within 4 hours of collection
• Customizable to use standard tube types
• White glove service: Phlebotomist who draws the blood hand-delivers it to the lab

Pricing
Pricing
When a client switches from Nationwide to Same-Day, it severely limits the patient pool we
have for that indication, which will decrease the prevelance and increase the Tier.
A Tier 1 disease can go to a Tier 5 with same-day.
We also add a $449 same-day delivery free. This is a higher price because:
• Phlebotomist could be driving hours for the delivery
• Operationally, they are more challenging
