Case Knowledge Check
Required
Match the correct definitions and responsibilities.
Correct Matches:
Case - a record logged anytime part of a process does NOT occur as intended
Stakeholder - Representative of a department who is in charge of reviewing cases for their
team
Project Manager - Responsible for recording all cases
QA - Responsible for reviewing and assigning risk ratings to cases based on their impact
Required
What is the main purpose of cases?
•
Identify trends to improve processes that aren't working
- The purpose is to improve processes, minimize challenges and replicate things that
are working well
Monitor KPIs
- Although some cases may impact KPIs, this is not the primary focus
Tattle tale
- The goal is not to finger point, but rather to help improve each teams function and
improve working conditions cross-functionally
