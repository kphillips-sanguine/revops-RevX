LiveSend
Livesend
Seismic’s LiveSend delivery enables users to share content with one or more buyers
using links that capture buyer engagement activity and report on it. LiveSend delivery
provides the following:
• Option to share a trackable link in an email to one or more buyers
• Option to generate a trackable link that can be shared by methods other than email
• Real-time alerts when recipients are viewing shared content
• Capability for buyers to share a LiveSend link with another contact, and for sellers to
track that recipient’s engagement separately
• Analytics that help sellers plan their next steps
• Reporting on out-of-the-box Seismic-managed and CRM-enhanced dashboards to
provide insights to other personas enterprise-wide

Digital Sales Room
With Digital Sales Rooms, sellers can create a personalized central location to share
content and collaborate with buyers, while capturing buyer engagement insights
throughout the sales cycle.
Digital Sales Rooms features include:
• An easy to use drag-and-drop design tool that allows sellers to deliver a custom
experience to buyers
• Co-branding and custom messaging, including custom video recordings
• Two-way communication including tagging and reactions
• Secure buyer login through email or LinkedIn for two-way communication
Buyer experience
Buyers can view a Digital Sales Room by following the room link that they received. When a

Functionality available to buyers in a Digital Sales Room include the following:

Sign in
Signing in is not required to view content in a Digital Sales Room. However, to comment or
engage in the conversation, buyers must create a Digital Sales Room account and sign in.
Additionally, signing in is required to access the User settings where buyers can customize
their profile, set comment notification alerts, and choose their privacy preferences.
Buyers have two options for signing in:
• Email: Create an account and sign in using their email address and a password.
• LinkedIn: Create an account and sign in using their LinkedIn profile credentials.
Commenting
Buyers and sellers can interact in a Digital Sales Room through comments in the
Conversation pane.

Search
What the Seismic search engine does
The Seismic search engine finds all documents that contain the keywords you choose to
characterize a document. For example, if you search for "case study telecom," the search
returns a list of all documents that contain all three keywords.
How the Seismic search engine looks for keywords
The search engine uses a query to find keywords in multiple locations within a document
including the following:
• Title, description, and body
• All content properties associated with a document
• Text found in images embedded in a document
• Audio transcription of videos
How search results are presented
When more than one content item matches the query, the list of matching documents is
ranked by relevance, or how well a particular document matches the query. More relevant
documents rank higher and appear toward the top of the search results, making it easier for
you to find them.
Along with results returned by the Seismic search engine, content managers can highlight
selected documents. These documents always appear at the top of the search results. This
feature allows organizations to customize their search experience and ensure that certain
documents are always visible to users.

Events
Events
Events are live, face-to-face interactions, delivered virtually or in-person, that help
organizations train employees and strengthen their professional competencies. Seismic
Learning doesn't host such events itself, but rather gives trainers a suite of tools for
tracking, managing, and promoting events.
With events, trainers can:
• Register attendees via pre-work lessons.
• Manage registrations and track event attendance.
• Assign attendees post-work after an event concludes.
This will be helpful so if you miss an event, you can still be watch the training and know the
follow up materials from that event. Everything will be housed in this one space, vs having
to watch for an email.
