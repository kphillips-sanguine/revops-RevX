Introduction to Gong Engage
Gong Engage is a feature within Gong. io's platform designed to enhance sales productivity
by integrating email and calendar systems to capture and analyze sales interactions
automatically. It tracks activities like emails and meetings, providing insights into prospect
engagement and the effectiveness of communication strategies. By optimizing sales
cadences and offering personalized recommendations, Gong Engage helps sales teams
tailor their approach, leading to improved engagement and better sales outcomes.
By transitioning to Gong Engage, Sanguine will be able to consolidate our tech stack and
gain improved transparency in active outreach strategy and pipeline. This will help provide
increased transparency into ongoing conversations and progress within specific accounts.
Additionally, AI integration will be able to make suggestions for which leads to target within
specific accounts based on previous successes and help increase personalization by
autogenerating emails.
What is the reason behind Sanguine Biosciences' decision to offboard SalesLoft and move
the Sales team to Gong Engage?
To help consolidate their tech stack and gain improved transparency in active outreach
strategy and pipeline.

Introduction to Chili Piper
As part of our transition to Gong Engage, Sanguine has gained access to Chili Piper's
advanced meeting scheduling platform. Chili Piper is designed to streamline the scheduling
process for sales teams. It automates the booking of meetings by integrating with calendar
systems and CRM tools, allowing prospects to book appointments directly through web
forms or emails. The platform ensures that meetings are scheduled at optimal times,
reducing no-shows and double bookings. Additionally, Chili Piper facilitates instant lead
routing and scheduling, enhancing efficiency and helping sales teams connect with
prospects faster, ultimately improving conversion rates and customer experience.
Chili Piper also allows for pre-built meeting types to be easily accessible for sales teams.
This is designed to efficiently standardize meeting scheduling best practices and naming
conventions for streamlined tracking. Through these meeting types, prospecting will
receive automated reminders for upcoming meetings, and pre-set meeting forms will allow
us to gather additional client information before meeting start times. Additionally, through
integration with Gong Engage's Consent pages, prospecting will have the ability to consent
to records before joining online meetings, increasing compliance and preventing the need
for covering this on live calls.
What is Chili Piper designed to do for sales teams?
Chili Piper is designed to streamline the scheduling process for sales teams by automating the
booking of meetings, integrating with calendar systems and CRM tools, and allowing
prospects to book appointments directly through web forms or emails.

Impact on Daily Operations
As we begin to move over to Gong Engage, you will still be able to finish out all Salesloft
activities for individuals working through cadences. We have timed this transition to allow
for ~2 months of access to both Gong Engage and SalesLoft.
This will allow us to efficiently finish all SalesLoft activities and have a backup as
continuous process improvements are put in place within Engage.
All new outreach is expected to be performed within Gong Engage.
Once access to Gong Engage is given, the expectation is to stop adding leads/contacts
into SalesLoft cadences and begin adding them into Engage flows.
Most cadences/templates have been created as flows within Gong Engage, but recently
created/new cadences and templates will need to be manually created in Gong Engage.
This process is very similar to creating cadences/templates within Salesloft and can be
performed quickly via the Copy/Paste function.
If existing cadences exceed the ~2-month period of access to Salesloft, they will
need to be manually removed from SalesLoft cadences and added into matching
Engage Flows.

Consent
Through Chili Piper, we have now set up a Gong Consent page for prospects joining our
recorded calls. This functionality allows for leads to either consent or not directly from the
meeting launch page before joining the call. If a lead consents to joining, they will join the
call as normal, but if they elect not to consent, the recorder will automatically be removed
when the prospect joins the calls.
This functionality helps improve compliance and prevents any awkward recording
announcements or conversations about removing the Gong recorder.

Completing To-Dos Best Practices for Efficiency:
To-Do's in Gong Engage are categorized based on the nature of the To-Do.
Ie. All, Emails, Calls, Linkedin, and Tasks.
To most efficiently move through our To-Dos, complete them based on the specific
type of To-Do rather than utilizing the “All” tab.
If we complete To-Dos based on the individual type of To-Do such as “Emails”, this will
automatically show the next To-Do similar to the functionality of SalesLoft.
If you go by “All” To-do's, you will have to click on each one individually to complete,
decreasing overall efficiency.
