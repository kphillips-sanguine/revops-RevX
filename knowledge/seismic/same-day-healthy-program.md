Overview
Same-Day Program
Sanguine has a same-day healthy program to deliver fresh whole blood from local, healthy
donors directly to the Client's lab on the day of collection. Sanguine has over 14,000
healthy donors within our community nationwide.
We deliver 5 days a week and identify donors within 4 hours of the client's labratory.
Same-Day coverage area includes Boston, Greater Philadelphia, Greater Washington DC,
Los Angeles, New York City, San Diego and San Francisco. Other locations

Healthy Control
What is a Healthy Control?
Sanguine will include participants who consider themselves to be "in general good health."
We exclude anyone with serious and Chronic Conditions/Co-morbidities.
Standard healthy control definition
• Sanguine will include participants who consider themselves to be "in general good
health". These participants may have common/mild health conditions that are generally
under control, including but not limited to: hypertension, high cholesterol, asthma,
anxiety, depression, ADD, ADHD, GERD, IBS, allergies, eczema, migraines, osteoarthritis,
sleep apnea, restless leg syndrome, eye issues (e.g. myopia, astigmatism, etc.). This may
also include participants who have previously been diagnosed, and have now recovered
from, COVID-19.
• Sanguine will exclude participants with any serious and chronic comorbidities. Examples
include: Systemic chronic autoimmune conditions such as lupus, rheumatoid arthritis,
Crohn's disease, ulcerative colitis; Any form of active cancer; Any chronic/active
infectious diseases (e.g. HIV, HCV, HBV); Diabetes type 1 and 2.
• Subjects in general good health may take NSAIDS irregularly or semi-regularly due to
conditions like: headache, body ache, cold/flu treatment and still be considered as long
as the medications are not being used for treatment of a major underlying condition.

Same-Day Study Designs
Scheduled Delivery:
Some clients know they need samples delivered on a specific day each week. If this is the
case, we can schedule patients for that day each week so they can expect blood when
they need it. This is the ideal same-day program set up because it provides consistency
and is a streamlined workflow for operations
Ad Hoc Delivery:
Some clients don't know when they will need samples and they need it on an ad-hoc basis.
If the client wants to go this route, we need to let them know we typically need a minimum
of 10 days notice prior to needing their sample. It is also helpful to ask if there can be 2
different options for delivery day in case their is trouble with scheduling for donor or
phlebotomist.
