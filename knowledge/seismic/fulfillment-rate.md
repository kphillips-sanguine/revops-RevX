Credibility
Credibility
We hear often from our clients that competitors say they will be able to find these patients,
or they will be able to find them in a specific time frame, and that time goes by but still no
delivery of their samples.
When you are talking about specific diseases within our community, we can use fulfillment
rate to prove our credibility and our ability to deliver on these samples. Whether it be that
specific indication or therapeutic area, saying we have a 95% fulfillment rate, including
various I/E criteria sends a strong message.
Bring this up when you are working on a study, in your capabilities presentation (when we
speak about our retention rate, also mention our fulfillment rate!) and again in your proposal
calls to really drive home the message that we deliver on our studies.

Community Access
Community Access
Whether we are pitching a community access model, or we are walking through the
specifics of our proposals, bringing up our fulfillment rate is crucial.
One of the biggest objections we get is, "What happens if we pay upfront and then you
can’t find the patients we need?" Typically we say something along the lines of, "We only
do community access for communities in which we have an engaged and a higher number
of patients, so that way we are able to handle various requests and I/E" now we can also
add:
Being able to back up our data, our community and our delivery is important to drive the
value home for these types of deals.
We have almost a 99% fulfillment rate for Crohn's disease across almost 35
different study designs"
